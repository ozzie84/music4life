WHAT IS MUSIC MEDICINE?

Music can reduce anxiety, anger, depression and grief as well as healing unsettledness!

Our bodies are polyrhythmical, they have heart rhythms, brain waves, breathing rhythms, hormonal & circadian rhythms. We walk in rhythm and talk in rhythm. So it should be no surprise that our bodies and minds respond to music. Music 4 Life has developed an evidence-based formula, based on music-therapy principles, for using music prescriptively to address various mood states and transition us to healthier mood states.
